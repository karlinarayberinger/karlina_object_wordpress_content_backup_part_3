(Starvation is an option, though I personally think it is a worse crime to surrender to the tyranny of an economy which enables wealth to be hoarded in amongst a disproportionately small number of humans and where not every human is guaranteed a home and sufficient food). I am not willing to "play by the rules" of a fucked up society at the expense of my health, freedom, and sense of ethics. I have so little to lose and so little to hope for given how unforgiving and unhelpful society is towards criminals. In the previous journal entry I wrote, I felt pressured by the military industrial complex to hurry up and say my last "words of weakness" before committing to an indefinitely long (at least ten years) period of hardly speaking and hardly asking anything of any other person (regardless of how much money I earn).
