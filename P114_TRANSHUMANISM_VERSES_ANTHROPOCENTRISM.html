TRANSHUMANISM_VERSES_ANTHROPOCENTRISM
PART_0: PREMISE In order for a human being to live as well as possible in the year 2021 and beyond, that human being must adopt a worldview which includes transhumanism as an essential component of what it means for human civilization to be worth sustaining and refining. Otherwise, that human being is settling for dying as a result of "expected normalized poverty" (and, as a corollary, living in anticipation of increasing mental and physical decline as that human being increases in age).
