After a citizen gives birth to two offspring, that citizen shall be permanently sterilized as soon as possible after its second offspring is born.

Such a sterilization procedure is arguably necessary because

(a) Planet Earth already contains more human organisms than it can sustain long-term (which means that the human species will almost certainly go extinct within the next 500 years if the total human population size does not shrink to a more sustainable number),
