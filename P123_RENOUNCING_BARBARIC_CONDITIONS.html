karbytes: “I disagree. I think that I am at least as mentally healthy as the humans who enjoy socializing with other humans more than I do. In fact, I daresay I am orders of magnitude happier on the inside because I have overcome what I think is one of the biggest scams throughout human evolution: the idea that human beings care about other human beings as anything more than tools to exploit for personal gratification. Because I spend significantly more time alone (and by choice) than the average human being does, I have enjoyed more opportunities to answer my own deepest philosophical questions and to research topics of interest without being told by other human beings how to make sense of things and what to focus my limited attention on.”

You may not like that fact about human nature, but if you want to minimize your suffering, you simply must comply with what your gang stalkers want.
